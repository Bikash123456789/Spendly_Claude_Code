Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login timestamps, used solely for security purposes.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate Spendly for you:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To respond to support requests if you contact us.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. No method of storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use minimal infrastructure services (such as hosting providers) to operate the app; these providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts. This request is made directly from your browser and is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
